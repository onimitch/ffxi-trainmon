General Notes

Regular Attacks are one of four Abilities:

  • Orb Toss: AoE damage and Bio. Removes Shadows.
  • Claw Attack: damage and Stun.
  • Orb Backhand: damage and Blind.
  • Kick: AoE damage and Knock Back.
