General Notes
  • Outdoors areas spawns only during Fog weather which is usually during the mornings 2:00-7:00
  • Indoors/Underground areas spawns occur all times of day with no weather requirement
  • When hit with elemental damage (including en-spells, etc) Djinns gain 1,000 TP.
    • This is useful for Beastmaster leveling as it causes them to blow up on beast pets for quick EXP.
