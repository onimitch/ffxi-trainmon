General Notes
  • Poison Breath is replaced with Incinerate for Eruca-type crawlers.
  • Eruca-type crawlers in outdoor Treasures of Aht Urghan areas sleep from 21:00 to 6:00 and are not aggressive or linkable when doing so.
  • Fire weather gives Eruca-type crawlers a very potent Regain effect.
